Promers stand for Professional Gamers. We are a group of gamers who have already represented INDIA in international level for DOTA (Defence of the Ancients), Player Unknown Battleground(PUBG) and Vainglory games and have earned regularly by streaming online. Now we have decided to help and trained intermediate gamers who are willing to pursue a gaming career. We support PUBG and Vainglory as for now. We recruit only intermediate player with the intention of framing them into world class renowned player. We teach tactics, strategy, skills, combo, counter build, team combination etc which is necessary to make you one of the dangerous player in the map. Moreover we organise regular meditation class inorder to relieve stress and pressure for all our register players. The most exciting part is every month we organised tournament within the register player and the winner/winner team recieve a Cash prize.
